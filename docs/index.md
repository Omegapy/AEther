This site is the public presentation layer for `The Æther-Flow Interpretation of Relativity`.

The main deliverable is the overview-first exact-closure package centered on [`aether_flow_exact_closure_sequence_overview.tex`](assets/tex/aether_flow_exact_closure_sequence_overview.tex). It presents an exact relativistic adoption of GR within the `Æther / Æther-flow` interpretation and keeps the claim boundary explicit: GR is adopted exactly at the operational level, while a completed first-principles substrate derivation remains open work.

## Claim Boundary

- `The Æther-Flow Interpretation of Relativity` is the active exact relativistic theory statement.
- The overview-first exact-closure sequence is the flagship public-facing package.
- GR is adopted exactly in the active package; it is not yet claimed as derived from substrate microphysics.
- The derivational bridge archive remains available for transparency and future work, but it is clearly downstream and optional.

For the fuller repository claim-boundary note, see [Æther-Flow Claim Boundary](AETHER_FLOW_CLAIM_BOUNDARY.md).
