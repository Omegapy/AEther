# Æther-Flow Claim Boundary

## Purpose

This note records the active claim boundary of `The Æther-Flow Interpretation of Relativity`.

- the `Æther` is the underlying four-dimensional substrate of reality
- the `Æther-flow` is the intrinsic ordered motion of that substrate
- observed three-dimensional space is a local experiential slice of the deeper substrate

This is an interpretive and structural claim. It is not a completed microphysical derivation of gravity.

- this is an adoption claim, not a first-principles substrate-derivation claim

The correct wording is:

- GR dynamics are adopted exactly within `The Æther-Flow Interpretation of Relativity`
