This page separates the broader research archive from the flagship exact-closure package.

The archive is included for transparency, continuity, and future work. It is not the main public deliverable, and it should not be mistaken for the current public theory claim.

## Reading Rule

Read the [Theory Package](theory-package.md) first. The archive is downstream and optional.

## What The Archive Contains

- The downstream derivational bridge sequence beginning with [`aether_flow_substrate_kinematics.tex`](../manuscripts/active/tex/aether_flow_substrate_kinematics.tex) and its later continuation through the active manuscript tree under `manuscripts/active/`.
- Technical screening, redesign, and closure notes that remain answerable to the exact-closure benchmark fixed by the flagship package.
- Older tested side branches archived in [`manuscripts/retired/`](../manuscripts/retired).

## How To Read The Archive Honestly

- Treat the archive as research transparency and optional foundational continuation.
- Do not treat the existence of many bridge manuscripts as evidence that a first-principles derivation of GR has already been completed.
- Keep the public claim boundary fixed: the active statement is exact relativistic adoption and exact closure, while deeper substrate derivation remains open.

## Why This Separation Exists

The repository now has a stable public-facing exact-closure package. Public presentation therefore needs a clean distinction between:

- the main theory statement that readers should evaluate first
- the larger derivational archive that remains valuable but secondary

That distinction is necessary for accessibility, transparency, and honest review.
